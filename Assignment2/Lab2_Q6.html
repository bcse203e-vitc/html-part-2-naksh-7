Recipe: Fried Rice

  • Home
  • Recipes
  • Tips

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Spring Onion,Beans,Garlic and Carrots
  • Chilli Sauce and Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Fry chopped garlic in a tablespoon of Groundnut oil.
  2. Now put the chopped carrots and beans in the pan and cook until they are soft.
  3. Now put in the sauces and salt according to taste.
  4. Finally mix the cooked rice and add spring onions and cook for two minutes.

Chef's Tips

The rice should be long and distinctive flavor.

Do not add the sauce directly to the oil.

Experiment with spices to enhance flavor!
